About LoopX: Our Story & Mission

Driving a sustainable future through accessible and rewarding recycling.

Why LoopX Matters

In a world grappling with increasing waste and environmental challenges, LoopX emerged with a clear vision: to **revolutionize recycling** by making it effortless, accessible, and rewarding for everyone. We believe that proper waste management isn't just a responsibility, but an opportunity to build a healthier planet and a more sustainable economy.

Based in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh, India, we understand the local challenges and are committed to creating a system that truly works for our communities, expanding our reach across India.

Our Mission

Empower Communities

To empower individuals and communities to embrace recycling by providing convenient pickup services and clear incentives.

Foster Sustainability

To foster a culture of sustainability and environmental responsibility through education and practical solutions.

Drive Circular Economy

To contribute significantly to the circular economy by diverting waste from landfills and ensuring materials are reused.
